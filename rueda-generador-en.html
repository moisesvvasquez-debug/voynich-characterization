What it shows — and what it doesn’t. That a few dials loaded with real frequencies often produce words that do exist in the manuscript (watch the counter) shows the text is a combinatorial system of three coupled slots, not a lexicon. What it does not show is what they mean: an empty generator and a meaningful catalogue produce the same forms. This is the machine, not the message.
